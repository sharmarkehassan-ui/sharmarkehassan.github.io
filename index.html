About Me

I'm a product manager in Toronto building at the intersection of AI and the businesses everyone ignores. GEO Scout measures AI search visibility for Canadian SMBs; CallFlow handles inbound calls for residential HVAC companies. Both came from the same instinct: find a gap, then close it. Before this, I spent 3+ years at CIBC in communications and public affairs, close enough to how large organizations make decisions to understand, more usefully, how they avoid making them.

I hold a CSPO from Scrum Alliance and a PM Certificate from York University. Most of my free time goes toward stress-testing whatever AI tool just shipped, or reading about problems I haven't thought about yet. I'm not someone who claims to have product figured out: I'm someone still early enough to be wrong about most things, and curious enough to keep going anyway.
